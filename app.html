Hexadecimal

Hex turns letters and numbers into number pairs.

Example: "Hello" becomes "48 65 6C 6C 6F"

Hex outputs your entire text and is case insensitive(outputs text exactly how you type them).

Ascii

Ascii gives every character a number.

Example: "Hello" becomes "072 101 108 108 111"

Ascii outputs your entire text and is case insensitive(outputs text exactly how you type them).

Binary

Binary reprensents text and numbers as 0's and 1's.

Binary outputs your entire string and is case sensitive(outputs text exactly how you type them).

Base64

Base64 represents text and numbers as Ascii text format it is case insensitive(outputs text exactly how you type them).

Example: "Hello" becomes "SGVsbG8="

Ensure that when decoding Base64 the hash should end with an equal to ("=") sign or two for efficiency.

A1Z26

A1Z26 gives every letter it position number in the alphabetical order (it reprensents spaces(" ") as 0).

Example: "Hello" becomes "8 5 12 12 15"

AIZ26 outputs your entire string and is case sensitive(outputs all text to uppercase).

Morse

Morse uses dots and dashes to reprensent text and numbers.

Example: "Hello" becomes ".... . .-.. .-.. ---"

When using our morse translator ensure you do not add punctuations they could lead to errors and the "/" after morse codes signify a word space it is case sensitive(only ouptuts upper case text).

Reverse

Reverse is simply text obfuscation (types text backward and is case insensitive).

Example: "Hello" becomes "olleH"

Atbash

Atbash is a substitution cipher it substitutes each letter with it miror (A = Z, B = Y).

Example: "Hello" becomes "Svool"

Caesar Cipher

Caesar is a substitution cipher it shifts letters by a fixed number down the alphabets or up all depending on the operation set.

CIPHERX: The Secret Message Playground

      
Engineered for speed, security, and simplicity. Developed by DAXTECH.


      

        
          Core Utilities & Live Experience
        
        

          
The engine of CipherX, designed for maximum speed and instant feedback.


          

            Live Output as You Type
            
See your translation results instantly as you type. This provides immediate
              feedback and zero waiting time. Requires a cipher option to be selected or Auto Assume enabled.

          


          

            Auto Detection
            
CipherX intelligently determines if your input is intended for encoding or
              decoding, significantly improving user experience and saving time.

          


          

            Ciphers
            
Access to 7+ basic ciphers. Select your desired cipher option from the home
              page and begin typing.

          


          

            Text to Speech
            
Hear your translated message spoken aloud for verification or accessibility.
            

          


          

            Voice Typing(Experimental)
            
Hands-free mode for input. Type without physically typing. (Under
                Development)

          

        

      


      


      

        
          UltX Mode: The Ultimate Security Layer
        
        

          
A proprietary cipher developed by DAXTECH. Text is encoded using a random sequence of ciphers, generating a
            unique encryption key.


          

            UltX Security
            
Your original message can only be viewed by recipients who possess both the
              key and the encoded message, and are using CipherX.

          


          

            Auto-Decryption Link
            
Links are generated 2-3 secs after typing 
When a link is shared, CipherX
              auto-fills the UltX form and instantly decodes
              the message upon opening.

            
Ensure you have a stable internet connection 

          


          

            Manual Super Decode
            
Allows manual input of an encoded message and its unique encryption key.
              (P.S.: Ensure you select your mode in the UltX forms for improved efficiency.)
            

          

        

      


      


      

        
          App Management & Engagement
        
        

          
Tools and features designed to make tracking, engaging, and sharing your activity seamless.

          

            History
            
Saves translation details (time, cipher type, operation, input, output).
              Auto-Save Trigger: History is saved when you switch cipher options or stop typing for 2-4 seconds.

          


          

            Stats
            
Data analyzed from your history to generate safe conclusions about your
              translation activity and habits.

          


          

            Achievements
            
Keep translating to earn achievements and make the experience fun!

          


          

            Easter Egg Reward
            
Unlock: Translate 15 times. Activation: Type reward/ to reveal
              the secret instructions. (A small green dot will appear on the homepage upon activation.)

          


          

            Sharing
            
Find multiple options available for sharing your output data, such as as a
              file or as plain text.

          

        

      


      


      

        
          Settings & Customization
        
        

          
Customize your CipherX experience and manage app state.


          

            Default Random Loader
            
CipherX by default loads a random loader each time you enter the app.

          


          

            Auto Assume
            
Set a preferred default cipher. This cipher will be assumed when you type
              without selecting an option first. (Under Development)

          


          

            Load App State
            
Saves and loads open tabs, last input, output, and selected options whenever
              you open the app again. (Under Development)

          


          

            Personalized Loader
            
Incorporates some of your user data into the loading animation. ⚠️ Warning:
              This greatly increases loader time.

          


          

            Auto Detect Network Quality
            
Future feature to optimize performance based on network speed. (TBD)
