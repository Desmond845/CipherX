Yes! CipherX uses industry-standard encryption algorithms and processes everything locally in your browser. Your data never leaves your device without encryption.

Can I use CipherX on mobile?

+

Absolutely! CipherX is fully responsive and works perfectly on smartphones, tablets, and desktop computers.

Are there any usage limits?

+

No, CipherX is completely free to use with no limits on the number of encryptions or messages.

All processing happens locally in your browser. Optional history storage uses your device's local storage and is never transmitted to our servers.
